fmb-mujoco

FMB (Functional Manipulation Benchmark) objects in MuJoCo: 54 pegs (9 shapes × 3 sizes × 2 lengths) matched to 3 hole boards, plus 3 interlocking assembly sets. Scenes ship with baked aligned keyframes and hybrid collision (CoACD hulls, hole-plugging hulls removed) so pegs really insert and are laterally constrained by real hole walls.

Code: github.com/joonhyung-lee/fmb-mujoco

Aligned — 360° sweeps

External disturbance

Random-direction forces applied to every free body (direction resampled at 30 Hz): 0.5 N horizontal, and 0.4×body-weight. The pegs rattle but stay seated — the hole walls really constrain the objects.
